What is an MU chart?

A MU chart stands for match up chart. Super Smash Brothers Ultimate has more than 70 characters with unique moves and playstyles. A match up chart is used to determine how a certain character would do against each other in a fair equally skilled fight within a competitive manner.

The following MU charts are made by various Link players. Keep in mind none of these are factual and are all based on opinion and personal experience
